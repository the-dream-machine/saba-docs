<Note>
  Saba works perfectly on the free version of Raycast. You do not need the paid
  Raycast Pro plan to access any features.
</Note>

## Channel Library

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }}
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dxe761tbf/image/upload/q_auto:good/v1687821647/documentation/Documentation-Channels.png"
/>
Get access to a growing library of 150+ live TV channels. Stream live TV in **720p**
at 60fps or **1080p** at 30fps. For a stable streaming experience, a minimum download
speed of **5Mbps** is recommended.

See the full list of channels [here](/guides/channels).

## Native Video Playback

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }}
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dxe761tbf/image/upload/q_auto:good/v1687723711/documentation/Documentation-Video-Players.png"
/>

Saba works by launching your preferred video player and streaming the selected live TV channel right in your video player.
Learn more about supported video players [here](/guides/video-players).

## Sports Schedule

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }}
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dxe761tbf/image/upload/q_auto:good/v1687864135/documentation/Documentation-Sports-Schedule.png"
/>

See all the events for major sports leagues in one view. Browse through a list of channels that are airing the game you want to watch and start streaming instantly.
If you missed a game, you can watch the highlights as soon as they are available.

## Movies & TV Shows Schedule

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }}
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dxe761tbf/image/upload/q_auto:good/v1687896168/documentation/Documentation-Movie-Schedule.png"
/>

A home cinema that's one key-press away. A guide for all the movies and TV shows that are playing across all the TV channels.
You can search for a movie or tv-show to find out what time it comes on. Search for a channel to see all the movie/shows airing on that channel during the day.

<Tip>
  Hover over a movie poster to see the movie's plot summary and year of release.
</Tip>

## Menu Bar Player

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }}
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dxe761tbf/image/upload/q_auto:good/v1687898041/documentation/Documentation-Menu-Bar.png"
/>

See what's playing right now at a glance. Expand the menu bar player to view upcoming shows and interact with player controls.

<Tip>
  Don't want people to see that you were just watching _The office_ before you
  started sharing your screen? You can hide the full title and keep the icon in
  place.
</Tip>

## Keyboard shortucts

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }}

You can set up convenient keyboard shortcuts to toggle the `next`/`previous` channel without opening Raycast or exiting your video player.

## Export channels

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }}
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dxe761tbf/image/upload/q_auto:good/v1687728604/documentation/Documentation-Platforms.png"
/>

Take the remote with you and stream on other devices. You can export a single link that contains all the channels.
Paste this link in one of the compatible apps for your phone or TV and import all the channels.

<Info>
  A detailed guide on the compatible apps for each platform will be available
  here soon.
</Info>

## Multiple languages supported

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }}
  src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dxe761tbf/image/upload/q_auto:good/v1687729204/documentation/Documentation-Languages.png"

Saba works reliably anywhere in the world with a stable internet connection. It also let's you tune in to multiple parts of the world and watch live TV in more than 30 languages.
